Permutation Test
Analysis of rs133381

rs133381 shows an observed ChIP-seq signal score of 2.602, which is significantly higher than expected by chance. The y-axis of the permutation histogram represents the frequency of permutations and the x-axis represents the ChIP-seq signal score. The green line (observed value) falls beyond the red significance threshold, indicating priority ($p = 1.94 \times 10^{-6}$). This SNP is ranked #4 by observed score and remains prioritized after the chromatin-state filter, passing the stringent FDR threshold of 0.0001 used in the analysis.
