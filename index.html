Analysis of rs133381

The green line (observed value) falls beyond the red significance threshold, indicating priority ($p = 1.94 \times 10^{-6}$). This SNP is ranked #4 by observed score and remains prioritized after the chromatin-state filter, passing the stringent FDR threshold of 0.0001 used in the analysis.
